## North star (decided 2026-07-02; proof order superseded 2026-07-13)

pm-substrate exists to make agent-run work more reliable by turning project-management state into an enforceable operational boundary. The product claim is causal and falsifiable: **on tasks that publicly expose agent-state failure, agents using pm-substrate must complete more of the benchmark's own tasks reliably than matched native and sham-sidecar controls, without hiding failures behind blocks, extra retries, or collateral state changes.** Internal events, receipts, and blocks diagnose the mechanism; they are never the outcome.

The eventual transfer targets remain the marketing lab (PluggedInSocial) and the hedge-fund lab (ArrowHedge), but they are frozen during the public-proof program. Neither app is used to design, tune, or score D6/D7. A public benchmark keep decision is required before app-boundary or business-operability work resumes. The executable proof protocol, failure modes, and thresholds are in [`docs/objective-falsification.md`](./docs/objective-falsification.md).

**Generic-first (decided 2026-07-06, chk_565b7eb9):** the substrate is NOT built for those two apps alone. Benchmark names and formats remain in peripheral adapters. No benchmark- or lab-specific code may enter `@pm/agent-state-core`; a missing general primitive may be added only after a public failure demonstrates the gap, and only with a non-test runtime consumer, ablation, and clean regression evidence.

**Falsification gate:** evidence maturity, not a calendar date, controls the decision. Qualification runs may debug the harness and set the confirmatory sample size, but cannot establish efficacy. The claim survives only after a held-out public benchmark shows task-level paired lift over both native and sham controls, anti-degenerate mutants fail, restart/dynamic-state guardrails hold, artifacts reproduce from a clean checkout, and the result replicates on a second benchmark or model. Otherwise repair, narrow, or kill the claim. If agents or evaluators route around the substrate, that is a failure.

## Hard requirements (non-negotiable product properties)

1. **Zero-rewrite integration.** A system adopts the substrate through a thin kit only: `mapping.yaml` (entity-mapping), a sync adapter (webhook/poll/CDC), an action executor (admitted action → the app's existing API). The substrate governs the **agent boundary**, never the app's internals. Proven pattern: ArrowHedge integrates as a vanilla upstream clone + read-only `/integration/v1/*` endpoints. CI-enforced on the substrate side by `validate:zero-edit`.
2. **Sidecar deployment.** Docker + Postgres in the user's environment (VPC or laptop). No hosted dependency, no data leaving.
3. **Agents integrate via protocol, not imports.** MCP tools (`resume`, `observe`, `propose`, `admit`, `checkpoint`) + HTTP. Kernel stays TypeScript (the type system is load-bearing); clients are language-agnostic; a thin Python client SDK ships when the labs need it.
4. **Control plane answers five questions** from the admitted log alone: What is being done? What did governance do (admitted / blocked / gated, by whom)? What did it cost (tokens, by session/agent)? What are the results (the eval metrics)? What got optimized (closed work + superseding decisions)? v0 is `pnpm dev:status`; v1 is the dashboard page.
5. **Adoption ramp is shadow-first.** Observe + warn with zero behavior change, produce the "what would have been blocked" report, then gate one action type at a time.
6. **No unconsumed primitives.** Every new export ships with a runtime consumer in the same change (the v62–v229 lesson; enforced by budgets/ratchet gates).
7. **Technical proof is not business proof.** Activity counts, fixture tests, and seeded failure blocks cannot by themselves satisfy “worth operating.” `pnpm pm:memo` applies the executable business-operability scorecard and a verdict ceiling from admitted evidence.
8. **Independent outcome oracle.** The benchmark-owned task oracle is primary and cannot import the substrate gate predicate. A block is success only when the unchanged upstream oracle says the task succeeded.
9. **Exact attempt identity.** Every result binds upstream URL and revision, license, task/split and corpus hashes, model/prompt/tool configuration, seed, substrate revision, arm, and unique attempt ID. Repeated attempts on one task are clustered, not counted as independent tasks.
10. **Placebo and anti-degenerate controls.** Compare native, equal-overhead sham sidecar, and real substrate arms. `block-all` and `allow-all` mutants, expected-allow cases, and irrelevant mutations must fail the qualification gate when appropriate.
11. **Authenticated, preregistered verification with separate decision authority.** Hashes, timestamps, signatures, and `independent=true` assertions are insufficient when the experiment caller selects their own trust policy. Decision tasks are the exact deterministic top-ranked subset of a predeclared eligible universe. Every D7 check references exactly one non-empty, kind-specific canonical observation; the gate binds its exact kind/check/subject and versioned procedure, validates strict fact keys, and rejects a signer-supplied result shortcut. The evidence is signed by an Ed25519 verifier whose owner differs from the experiment producer; the manifest is preregistered before execution; attempt times/raw roots are separately attested; and verifier keys/source revisions come from a policy pinned outside the result bundle. Crucially, adapter-specific procedures must derive the structured facts from the bound manifest, attempts, analysis, and content-addressed raw benchmark records. Until then, internally consistent signed facts are diagnostic only and the report must be `not_eligible`. Only after that derivation boundary exists may automated verification report conditional evidence eligibility. An authenticated owner must still separately authorize the exact report hash before any KEEP or app-unfreeze consequence.

### Up next — public-proof execution sequence

The implementation/execution distinction and exact provider blockers are
recorded in [`docs/public-benchmark-status-2026-07-13.md`](./docs/public-benchmark-status-2026-07-13.md).
As of that snapshot, each of the six steps has a concrete implementation or
research artifact, but several intentionally refuse to produce eligible
evidence. ToolSandbox stopped on provider quota and lacks a real sidecar/provider
receipt bridge; STATE-Bench lacks official credentials and raw judge/provider
receipts; the corner battery has qualified plumbing and a hardened harness but
no real matched-agent execution; and no externally anchored preregistration,
replication bundle, or owner authorization exists. The causal claim remains
unproven.

1. **Evidence integrity reset.** Land the public-evaluation manifest, independent receipt verifier, exact attempt/pair identities, native/sham/substrate arms, outcome-first KPIs, and anti-degenerate controls. Re-label all existing local results as conformance-only.
2. **ToolSandbox vertical slice.** Reproduce the pinned multi-turn cellular/message task unchanged, then run a labeled lost-response/process-restart derivative. Validate strict completion, duplicate sends, collateral state, latency, and cost from raw artifacts.
3. **STATE-Bench qualification then confirmatory run.** Pin the official Agent Learning Track, lock the held-out boundary, use the official judge, predeclare exclusions and sample size from qualification data, then run the untouched confirmatory set once per declared seed/model run.
4. **Corner battery.** Add independent public scenarios for fact supersession, two-step restart/idempotency, and changing-world state. Run expected-allow/block and irrelevant/ABA/concurrency/authority perturbations without changing the benchmark's headline task definitions.
5. **Arrowsmith repair loop.** Classify only observed failures against existing primitives. Research and implement the smallest general repair, prove it has a real runtime consumer, ablate it, and rerun the exact failed case plus clean controls.
6. **Replicate and decide.** Externally preregister the manifest, verify receipts from a clean checkout, replicate across a second benchmark or model, and report task-level paired confidence intervals, reliable success, false-block/collateral rates, and cost per strict success. The evaluator reports conditional evidence eligibility only. A separately authenticated owner then signs the exact report hash to keep, repair, narrow, or kill before any app work resumes.

## Anti-drift rules for this file

Roadmap changes are `decision` checkpoints first, edits here second. Until a separately authenticated D7 owner decision exists, do not modify or use PluggedInSocial or ArrowHedge as efficacy evidence. Benchmark names belong only in peripheral adapters. The official benchmark oracle must remain independent of substrate gating, and untouched headline results must remain separate from derivative fault-injection results. Research may propose; only observed-failure-driven, runtime-consumed code counts toward a causal repair. No event, block, receipt, retry, local-fixture pass, or self-selected trust policy may proxy for strict public task completion, reliability, collateral effects, cost, replication, or decision authority.
